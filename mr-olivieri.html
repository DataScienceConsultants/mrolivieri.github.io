01 / THE ARTIST

Built from the streets, painted with identity.

Humberto Olivieri, known artistically as Mr. Olivieri, creates work that carries the texture of the island — expressive lines, saturated color, cultural symbolism, and the pulse of the city.

His pieces feel alive: part mural, part memory, part movement. Each work becomes a conversation between place, people, and pride.
