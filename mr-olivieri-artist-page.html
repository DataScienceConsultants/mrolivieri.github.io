About The Artist

Color, culture, and storytelling in public form.

Mr. Olivieri creates visual experiences that connect people to place. His work carries the energy of Puerto Rico through bold color, expressive composition, and meaningful cultural references.

From public murals to commissioned artwork and creative collaborations, his practice celebrates identity, memory, rhythm, and the visual language of Borinquen.
